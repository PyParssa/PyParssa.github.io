FAQ

Your questions, answered.

Will my site go down while you work on it? +

No. We work on a staging environment first, then deploy the final version. Your live site stays up the entire time. If anything unexpected happens, we fix it immediately at no extra cost — that's our no-downtime guarantee.

How does the AI chatbot have no monthly fee? +

Most chatbot platforms charge monthly because they host the AI on their servers and profit from the subscription. We deploy a client-financed model: the infrastructure runs on your hosting (or a low-cost server you own). You pay once for setup, then nothing ongoing. The AI model itself uses open or cost-efficient API calls that typically cost pennies per month — not hundreds of dollars.

How long does the full revival take? +

Most revived sites are fully live within 5–7 business days. We'll give you a specific timeline after reviewing your site — some sites need more work than others, and we'll be upfront about that before you pay anything.

Do I need to understand any of this technical stuff? +

Not at all. We handle 100% of the work. You give us access to your WordPress dashboard and hosting, and we do everything else. We'll keep you updated at each step in plain language, not tech jargon.

What languages will my site support? +

We'll discuss your target markets during onboarding, but we can support any major language including Arabic, Farsi, French, Spanish, German, and more. The AI chatbot also responds in the visitor's language automatically.

What if I'm not happy with the result? +

We do a full review call before the site goes live. If something isn't right, we fix it before launching. Our goal is a site you're proud of — we don't consider the job done until you're satisfied.

How It Works

A quiet ritual, not another feed.

01

Build your sitting

Choose one to five questions to hold each day. Small and repeatable beats sweeping and rare.

02

Answer honestly

Write for yourself first. Publish only if it might help someone find their own way through.

03

Watch the pattern form

A year of sittings becomes a quiet portrait — a record of who you were becoming, and when.

04

Send one along

Some questions aren't meant to be kept. Send one to someone you love, and see what comes back.

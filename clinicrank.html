FAQ

Common questions.

How is GEO different from traditional SEO? +

Traditional SEO gets you ranked on Google's blue links. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) gets your clinic cited inside AI-generated answers on ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and similar tools. As more patients use AI to find healthcare providers, GEO is becoming as important as traditional SEO — and right now, almost no clinics are doing it.

How long before I see results? +

Most clients see measurable ranking improvements within 60–90 days. Significant traffic and lead increases typically follow by months 3–6. SEO is not instant — but unlike ads, the results compound and continue growing long after you've paid for the work. Think of it as an asset, not an expense.

Is this HIPAA compliant? +

We are an SEO/GEO service, not a software platform that stores patient data — so HIPAA directly governs your systems, not ours. That said, all content we produce is reviewed against medical content compliance standards (E-E-A-T, YMYL), and we never recommend strategies that would put your practice at regulatory risk. We always advise working with your legal counsel on specific compliance questions.

Do you work with all types of clinics? +

We work with medical clinics (dental, dermatology, cardiology, general practice, cosmetic surgery, etc.), allied health practices, and health tech companies. We take on a limited number of new clients per month to ensure quality — which is why we recommend booking your audit before slots fill.

What's included in the free audit? +

Your free audit includes: a full technical SEO health check, current keyword ranking analysis, competitor gap report, local search visibility score, AI citation check (are you mentioned in ChatGPT/Perplexity?), and a prioritized action plan. It's a real deliverable — not a sales pitch dressed up as a report.

What's your guarantee exactly? +

If you don't see measurable improvements in keyword rankings and organic patient inquiries within 90 days of your campaign going live, we continue working at no charge until you do. This applies to clients on our Authority and Dominate plans. We put our work on the line because we're confident in it.
